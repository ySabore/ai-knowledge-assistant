Aurora Facilities Group
Supplier Standards

Purpose
Aurora uses these standards to evaluate vendors that provide facility supplies, specialty trades, access hardware, uniforms, safety equipment, and emergency response support.

Baseline requirements
- valid business registration
- tax documentation on file
- certificate of insurance meeting contract minimums
- agreement to Aurora code of conduct and confidentiality expectations
- ability to meet invoicing and service documentation requirements

Operational expectations
- Vendors must arrive during the approved service window unless dispatch authorizes an exception.
- Vendors must follow customer sign-in, escort, badge, and safety rules.
- Technicians and drivers must present themselves professionally and avoid discussing unrelated customer issues on site.
- Vendors are expected to notify Aurora immediately if they cannot meet a committed arrival window.

Performance standards
- on-time arrival target: 95 percent
- service completion documentation submitted within one business day
- invoice accuracy target: 98 percent
- safety incident rate reviewed quarterly

Disqualifiers
- repeated missed appointments without notice
- falsified service records
- failure to maintain insurance
- unsafe on-site conduct
- unauthorized subcontracting

Preferred supplier signals
- strong geographic coverage
- responsive dispatch communication
- clear field documentation
- consistent pricing
- proven performance on occupied and security-sensitive sites
