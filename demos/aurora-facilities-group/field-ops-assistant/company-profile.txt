Aurora Facilities Group
Company Profile

Overview
Aurora Facilities Group is a regional facilities services provider that supports office towers, mixed-use campuses, healthcare administration buildings, and light industrial properties. Aurora combines on-site field teams with centralized dispatch, vendor coordination, and account management.

Service lines
- Day porter and janitorial coordination
- Building access support and after-hours unlock response
- Preventive maintenance coordination
- Minor repair dispatch
- Vendor escorting and site readiness checks
- Incident response and building status reporting

Operating model
- Every customer site is assigned an account manager and a site lead.
- After-hours issues route to the central dispatch desk first.
- Field technicians are dispatched based on skill set, location, customer priority tier, and badge eligibility.
- Major incidents require both operational response and customer communication updates.

Priority tiers
- Priority 1: life safety, active water leaks, power loss in critical areas, building access failure at occupied sites
- Priority 2: HVAC disruption in occupied areas, restroom outages, loading dock access issues, alarm coordination
- Priority 3: non-urgent repairs, cosmetic issues, routine supply requests, scheduled follow-up work

Escalation expectations
- Priority 1 incidents must be acknowledged immediately and escalated to the on-call manager within 10 minutes.
- Priority 2 incidents must be acknowledged within 30 minutes.
- Customer-visible disruptions require status updates until resolution or formal handoff.

Customer communication
- Site teams should use clear, factual language and avoid speculative root-cause statements.
- When resolution timing is uncertain, technicians should share the next checkpoint time rather than guessing an ETA.
- Sensitive security details should only be communicated to approved customer contacts.
