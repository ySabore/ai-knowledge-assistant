Summit Family Medicine — Medication Refill Policy (Demo)

Routine refills
- Stable chronic medications: up to three 90-day refills per year without visit when clinically appropriate per provider review.

Controlled substances
- No automatic refills; appointment required at least every 90 days for schedule II–IV as per state law and clinic policy.

Pharmacy
- E-prescribe preferred; fax requests processed within 2 business days.
