Summit Family Medicine — Clinical Protocols (Demo — Not Medical Advice)

Preventive care cadence
- Adults 18–64: annual wellness visit; blood pressure and BMI each visit.
- Diabetes follow-up: A1C every 3 months when treatment changes or not at goal.

Acute same-day visit
- Triage nurse callback within 30 minutes during business hours for urgent concerns.

After-hours
- Nurse line routes to on-call physician; emergency symptoms directed to 911 per script.
