Summit Family Medicine — Scheduling FAQ (Demo)

Appointments
- New patients: first available within 14 days for primary care; same-week for urgent slots released daily at 8:00 AM.

Cancellations
- Cancel or reschedule at least 24 hours in advance to avoid no-show fee ($35 for non-Medicare visits per policy).

Telehealth
- Follow-up for select conditions; video link sent 15 minutes before appointment.

Interpreter services
- Spanish and ASL available with 48-hour notice; other languages via phone interpreter.
