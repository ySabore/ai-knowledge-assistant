Summit Family Medicine — Notice of Privacy Practices (Demo — Summary)

Uses and disclosures
- We use health information for treatment, payment, and health care operations as permitted by HIPAA.

Your rights
- Right to access, amend, and request restrictions on certain disclosures; right to an accounting of disclosures in limited circumstances.

Complaints
- Contact our Privacy Officer; you may also file with the U.S. Department of Health and Human Services.

Breach notification
- We notify affected individuals without unreasonable delay and within 60 days when required.
