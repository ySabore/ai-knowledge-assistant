Sterling & Vale LLP — Conflicts of Interest Policy

Screening
- All new matters run through conflicts database before engagement letter issuance.

Imputation
- Conflict for one attorney is imputed to the entire firm unless ethically screened under applicable rules and client consent obtained.

Business conflicts
- Representing competitors in unrelated matters may require informed consent from both clients when material risk exists.

Waivers
- Documented written waivers retained in matter file for seven years.
