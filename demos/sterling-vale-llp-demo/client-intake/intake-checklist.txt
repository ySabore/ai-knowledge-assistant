Sterling & Vale LLP — New Matter Intake Checklist

Initial contact
- Capture conflict wall clearance before sharing confidential facts.
- Verify client identity and authority to retain the firm.

Engagement letter
- Scope of representation, fee arrangement, and termination clause must be signed before substantive advice on the matter.

Urgent matters
- Criminal or imminent-deadline matters: same-day attorney callback per rotating intake partner.

Documentation
- Corporate clients: certificate of good standing and beneficial ownership when required by firm policy.
