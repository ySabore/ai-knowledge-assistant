Sterling & Vale LLP — Discovery Guidelines

Hold and preservation
- Litigation hold notice within 24 hours of reasonably anticipating litigation; refresh quarterly.

Production format
- Native production preferred; TIFF + load file when court order requires.

Privilege log
- Log withheld items within 14 days of production unless court order differs; include Bates range and succinct description.

Meet-and-confer
- Good-faith meet-and-confer required before motion to compel absent emergency.
