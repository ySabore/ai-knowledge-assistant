Northwind Retail — Incident Reporting (LP)

Theft or fraud
- Document time, location, associate names, and customer description without profiling language.
- Internal theft: escalate to Regional LP same day; do not confront alone.

Safety first
- No physical apprehension; observe and report. Call 911 when there is threat of violence.

System use
- Enter incidents in LP case tool within 4 hours; attach CCTV reference numbers when available.
