Northwind Retail — Loss Prevention Standards

Floor awareness
- Greet customers within 10 feet; maintain line of sight to high-shrink fixtures.

High-shrink categories
- Electronics accessories, premium apparel, and health/beauty require spider-wrap or locked cases per planogram.

Bag checks
- Associates may request receipt verification for unbagged merchandise leaving POS; follow local law and HR script.

Partnering with law enforcement
- Only Regional LP or store manager may share video; preserve chain of custody on exports.
