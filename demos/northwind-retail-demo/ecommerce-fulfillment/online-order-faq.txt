Northwind Retail — Online Order FAQ

Order changes
- Address changes allowed only before fulfillment starts; contact support within 1 hour of order placement when possible.

Cancellations
- Full cancellation with refund if no items have shipped; partial cancellations may adjust promotions.

Damaged or missing items
- Report within 48 hours of delivery with photos; replacement or refund per policy.

BOPIS (buy online, pickup in store)
- Ready notifications via SMS/email; bring ID and order number.
